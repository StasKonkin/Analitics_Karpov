This time we have a dataset about advertisements

Here is the data of interactions with ads on a certain site over a period of 6 days. And a table with the characteristics of the ad clients (those who placed these ads).

ads_data.csv - data of interactions with ads
date - date
time - time
event - action (view/click)
platform - platform
ad_id - id of ad
client_union_id - id of the advertising account
campaign_union_id - id of the advertising campaign
ad_cost_type - payment type
ad_cost - price
has_video - whether there is video
target_audience_count - size of audience

ads_clients_data.csv - characteristics of ad clients
date - date
client_union_id - id of ad clients
community_id - id of community
create_date - date of creation of advertising client
